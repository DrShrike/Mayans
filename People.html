Maya People

The Ancient Maya were a sophisticated culture that established their cities in the rainforest and were famous for their knowledge of the stars. They were fascinated by the idea of time and developed advanced astronomy, calendar systems, and writing. The Maya chewed gum, filed their teeth, and raised bees without stingers. They built elaborate pyramids, often adorned with murals and intricate architectural details, which are similar to the pyramids built by the Egyptians.
